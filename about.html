🌱 Our mission

Deep ecology, deeper knowledge

We believe that gardening is a practice of attention. Our mission is to share practical, evidence-based wisdom that helps growers of all scales build resilience — in their soil, their communities, and themselves.

We avoid quick fixes and instead focus on regenerative systems: no-dig, permaculture, heirloom varieties, and ecological design.

📖 Our philosophy

Slow gardening for a changing world

Zephoria Living Roots is a space for curiosity, not certainty. We embrace the messiness of nature, the joy of experimentation, and the humility of learning from failure.

We are committed to inclusivity, accessibility, and the belief that everyone deserves a patch of earth to tend.

🧑‍🌾 The editor

Mara Solis

Mara is a horticulturist, writer, and restless experimenter. She spent a decade managing organic farms before trading acres for a half-acre urban food forest in the Pacific Northwest. Her work has appeared in Modern Farmer and Permaculture Magazine.

She believes that gardening is a form of storytelling, and that the best stories are written in collaboration with the land.

🌿 Contributors

Growers, thinkers, tinkerers

Our contributors include soil scientists, herbalists, agroecologists, and home gardeners who bring diverse perspectives. We prioritise voices from under-represented communities and celebrate the many ways people connect with the living world.
