🍪 Cookies Policy

We keep it light and transparent — just like a perfect shortbread. Here’s everything you need to know about cookies on Zephoria Living Roots.

last updated: june 2026

🍪 What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you interact with our content, and improve your experience. Think of them as tiny garden markers that help us tend to your visit.

📋 How we use cookies

  • Essential cookies: These are strictly necessary for the website to function properly (e.g., remembering your cookie preferences).
  • Preference cookies: We use a minimal cookie to remember your language or region settings if you choose them.
  • Analytics cookies: We use a privacy-friendly analytics tool (no personal data, no tracking across sites) to count visits and see which pages are popular.
  • We do not use advertising or tracking cookies from third-party networks.

🔧 Your control

  • You can manage or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings.
  • Most browsers allow you to block all cookies, but this may affect your experience on some websites.
  • We provide a simple cookie banner on your first visit so you can choose your preferences.

📊 Third-party cookies

We do not use third-party cookies for advertising or social media tracking. Any embedded content (like videos) may set their own cookies, but we encourage you to review those providers’ policies.

🌱 Our promise

We never sell or share your data through cookies. We believe in respectful, ethical digital gardening — where your privacy is as important as the soil we tend.

📅 Changes to this policy

This Cookies Policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with the effective date. We recommend reviewing it from time to time.

Cookies are just digital seeds — we use them sparingly and with care. 🌿
