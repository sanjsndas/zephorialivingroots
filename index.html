    ❓ Frequently asked questions

    What is no-dig gardening?

    A method that builds soil from the top down without tilling, preserving fungal networks and soil structure. We have a full 10-part series.

    How do I start a food forest on a small lot?

    Begin with a guild: one tree, plus shrubs, herbs, and groundcovers. Our 'Forest Garden Layers' article is a great entry point.

    Do you recommend organic fertilizers?

    Yes, but we focus on building soil organic matter first. Fertilizers are a supplement, not a foundation.
